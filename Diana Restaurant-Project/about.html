Our Story

Diana Restaurant | Nyendo, Masaka City | Owner: Ampaire Diana Ritah | Contact: 0753434801

Diana Restaurant is a dream come true for Ampaire Diana Ritah, who turned her passion for authentic Ugandan cuisine into a warm, welcoming space for all. Her journey began in Nyendo, Masaka City, inspired by family traditions and a love for sharing delicious food with the community.

From Humble Beginnings

Diana Restaurant started as a small family venture in Nyendo, Masaka City, born from Diana's deep love for traditional Ugandan cuisine and her vision to create a place where authentic flavors are celebrated and shared.

Our founder, Ampaire Diana Ritah, grew up learning traditional cooking methods from her family, believing that food is more than sustenance—it's a way to connect people, preserve culture, and create lasting memories.

Today, Diana continues this legacy by sourcing ingredients from local farmers, preparing dishes using time-honored techniques, and serving them in an atmosphere that feels like home. Every meal carries the warmth of Ugandan hospitality and the authenticity of Diana's personal touch.
